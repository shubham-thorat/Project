    Safety Measure

  1. Buses will stop only as designated bus stops
  2. If a large number of passengers are noticed at any bus stop, the crew must inform the depot manager about the same.
  3. The driver, conductor and all other bus crew must wear face masks
  4. Crew will be allowed to board the bus o after their body temperature is checked
  5. Crew must use sanitizers
  6. Buses must be cleaned, sanitized and disinfected before deployment
